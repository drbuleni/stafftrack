About KPI Categories
  • Active categories will appear when scoring staff KPIs each week.
  • Inactive categories won't appear in scoring forms but historical scores are preserved.
  • Role assignment - Assign categories to specific roles (e.g., "Sterilization compliance" for Dental Assistants only). Leave empty to apply to all roles.
  • Deletion - Categories with existing scores cannot be deleted. Deactivate them instead.
